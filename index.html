Our motivation

The Caltech Data Science Organization is led by a dedicated team of students advised by professors Yaser Abu-Mostafa and Yisong Yue.

We tell stories with data.

Our members' projects include tools for policy-makers to prevent malaria (1st place and $100,000 at the Citadel Data Open Championship), novel quantum machine learning algorithms (invited talks at Stanford and KAIST), a browser plugin to fight fake news (featured in The New York Times, The Washington Post, Fox News, and NPR) and Kaggle projects placing in the top 1%.

Our path

Creating a community of data scientists.

We seek to connect, inspire, and support students across Caltech.
